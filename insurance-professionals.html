How We Work

Our Process

A clear, predictable workflow designed for seamless coordination with your claims process.

1

Emergency Dispatch

24/7 dispatch by phone or online form. Your assigned project manager is your single point of contact from the first call through project close-out.

2

On-Site Assessment & Scope

Thorough assessment with moisture mapping, thermal imaging, and photo documentation. A detailed scope of work is prepared and shared before any mitigation begins.

3

Mitigation & Stabilization

Emergency mitigation to prevent secondary damage. Structural drying, containment, water extraction, board-up, and hazard control as required by the loss.

4

Documentation & Reporting

Daily progress reports, moisture readings, photo logs, and air quality data delivered digitally. All documentation formatted to insurance industry standards for clean claim processing.

5

Restoration & Reconstruction

Full restoration from demolition through rebuild, managed by the same team. No subcontractor handoffs, no delays between mitigation and repairs.

6

Final Walkthrough & Sign-Off

Joint walkthrough to confirm all work meets specifications. Complete project file with all documentation, reports, and sign-off delivered for your records.
