Insurance

We handle the insurance.
You handle recovery.

We work directly with your insurance company, document everything to their standards, and manage the entire claims process so you can focus on getting back to normal.

The Process

How It Works

1

Call Us

You call, we dispatch. Our team arrives quickly to assess the damage, secure the property, and begin emergency mitigation to prevent further loss.

2

Documentation

We document everything to insurance standards with photos, moisture readings, thermal imaging, and detailed reports. This thorough documentation is what gets claims approved.

3

Insurance Liaison

We submit directly to your insurer and handle all communication. Our team speaks the language adjusters expect and provides the documentation they need to process your claim efficiently.

4

Restoration Begins

Once approved, we start work immediately. One team manages your project from demolition through reconstruction. No subcontractors, no handoffs, no delays.

5

Final Walkthrough

We walk through the completed restoration with you to ensure everything meets your expectations. Your satisfaction and the quality of the work are confirmed before we close the project.
